Our foundation activities

  • 2010

    Week Camp

    It's a special camp where participants can experience the Football program intensively for a week -Sunday to Friday, 5 hours a day. In this camp importance is placed on speaking to the participants in the English language during soccer practices and a leadership session is held that is accompanied by some practical exercises during the week of the camp.

  • 2015

    Id Camp

    This is a Talent Identification Camp for members of the Football program, participants of the WEEK-CAMP Football and the public. This Camp during 4 days the participants of the Football program, participants of the WEEK-CAMP Football and public will have the opportunity to participate in training sessions and soccer practices in small spaces.
